How to play:

Objective:
  • Be the first to sink all 5 of your opponent's ships!
Ship Placement:
  • Each player begins by placing 5 ships on the board.
  • Ships can be placed horizontally or vertically
  • Ships cannot overlap nor be placed outside of the board.
Naval Combat:
  • Each player takes turns attacking a coordinate on the board.
  • If an opponent's ship is hit, the cell will turn red.
  • If an attack misses, the cell will turn white.
